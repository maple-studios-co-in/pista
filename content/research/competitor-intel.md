## fudr — business.fudr.in

- **Positioning (their words):** "AI Powered Loyalty & Engagement Platform for Restaurant Chains." Hero: "GROW YOUR LOYAL CUSTOMERS BY 3X — Boost your Brand's Customer Retention, Experience and Revenue."
- **Product modules:** Loyalty Engine (composable loyalty program), Redemption across Channel (in-store + app), Guest Feedback, Personalised Marketing (data-driven campaigns across channels), Connected Customer Journeys (streak offers), Actionable Insights, Automated Rewards, Frictionless Ordering & Table Reservation. Method framed as: Create Touchpoints (brand's own Loyalty App; collect data from WhatsApp & Instagram) → Generate Personas (recency/frequency/spend) → Launch Targeted Campaigns.
- **Pricing:** Not shown (demo-request only).
- **Target customer:** Leading restaurant / F&B chains (multi-store brands, coffee chains).
- **Notable claims / logos:** "1,05,43,000+ Customer Relationship Served," "12x Increase in Repeat Business," "30% Increase in Customer Spend," "2.5M+ Loyalty Members." Marquee clients/testimonials: Barista Coffee (Rajat Agarwal, CEO), Nothing Before Coffee / NBC (Anand Jain), Massive Restaurants (Zorawar Kalra). Operated by December Technologies Pvt Ltd; backed by Expert Dojo per media coverage.
- **India focus:** Yes (Jaipur-based; F&B SaaS for Indian chains).

_Source: https://business.fudr.in/_

## Petpooja — petpooja.com (+ /pricing)

- **Positioning (their words):** "All-in-One Software Powering Business Growth At Every Step"; home billed as "Software for Small and Medium Businesses." For restaurants: "Restaurant POS software made simple!"
- **Product modules:** POS (POSS) — billing, inventory, online ordering, reporting (80+ reports), menu, in-built CRM; Payroll (with biometric hardware); Invoice (GST billing for B2B/retail); Tasks (operational task management). Add-ons on pricing page: Kitchen Display System, Captain Ordering App, Restaurant Website, Petpooja Loyalty, Token Management, Feedback App/QR, Scan & Order, Self-Service Kiosk, SMS Service, Order Reconciliation, Analytics & Insights.
- **Pricing:** Pricing page has three tiers — **Core / Growth ("Popular") / Scale** — billed monthly or annually, but the actual price figures render as images/JS and are **not shown as numbers** in the page source. Stated as "industry-low, transparent pricing… No hidden fees."
- **Target customer:** SMEs and restaurant chains; outlet types span fine dine, QSR, cafe, food court, cloud kitchen, bakery, bar & brewery, pizzeria, large chains.
- **Notable claims / logos:** "Trusted by 1,50,000+ businesses across the globe," "14+ years of excellence," "0% processing errors." Logos: Haldiram's, Zepto, Bira, L&T Realty, La Pino'z, Hocco, Jumbo King. Testimonials: Burgerama, Yangkiez Momos, Berco's, Sam's Pizza (90+ outlets), The Belgian Waffle Co.
- **India focus:** Yes (Ahmedabad-based, Prayosha Food Services), with global reach.

_Sources: https://www.petpooja.com/ ; https://www.petpooja.com/poss/pricing_
